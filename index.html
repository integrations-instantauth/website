How InstantAuth Works

InstantAUTH modernized patient care from the first phone call to the office.

Agentic Intake

During intake or provider encounter, InstantAUTH immediately determines whether prior authorization is necessary.

Intelligent Analysis

If necessary, InstantAUTH automatically completes payor portal questions, even drawing necessary documentation from the EMR, and obtains approval.

Automated Prior Authorization

If certain prior treatments are required for PA, InstantAUTH documents accordingly or notifies provider of necessary steps based on up to date payor guidelines.
