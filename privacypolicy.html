Privacy Policy

Overview

At InstantAUTH, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we gather, utilize, and safeguard your personal data to ensure a seamless experience on our platform.

Data We Gather
  • Personal Information: Includes your name, email, and phone number.
  • Usage Data: Insights into how you interact with our platform.
  • Cookies and Tracking: Information about your browsing habits.
Purpose of Data Usage
  • Enhance our services.
  • Communicate with you, including promotional messages.
  • Personalize your experience.
  • Fulfill legal requirements.
Disclosing Your Information
  • To service providers aiding our operations.
  • To third parties when legally necessary or to safeguard our interests.
Your Rights and Options
  • Access, update, or delete your information.
  • Opt-out of marketing messages.
  • Adjust cookie settings via your browser.
Protecting Your Information

We implement industry-standard security practices to safeguard your data. However, keep in mind that no online transmission or storage method is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy Policy

Our services are not intended for those under 13 years old. We do not intentionally collect personal data from children.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this policy from time to time. Please check back for any changes.
